Energy Flow Polynomials (EFPs) are a set of observables, indexed by non-isomorphic multigraphs, which linearly span the space of infrared and collinear safe (IRC-safe) observables.

An EFP index by a multigraph $G$ takes the following form: where $z_i$ is a measure of the energy of particle $i$ and $\theta_{ij}$ is a measure of the angular separation between particles $i$ and $j$. The specific choices for energy and angular measure depend on the collider context and are discussed at length in the Measures section.
